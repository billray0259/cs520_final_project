What is RouteRater?

Elevating indoor route ratings and progress tracking.

RouteRater is a web app revolutionizing indoor climbing by improving route ratings. It utilizes QR codes for climbers to provide accurate difficulty ratings, leading to consistent and reliable assessments.

RouteRater offers personal progress tracking, allowing climbers to monitor their skill development over time. With RouteRater, climbers can enhance their climbing experience and make informed decisions based on reliable route ratings and their own growth.
